Key Components

  • Article management system with rich text editor
  • Category and tag organization system
  • Advanced search with filters and full-text search
  • Comment system with moderation tools
  • Subscription and paywall management
  • Author profiles and contributor management
  • RSS feed generation and distribution
  • Newsletter subscription and management
  • Social media integration and sharing
  • Media gallery and video embedding
  • Related articles and content recommendations
  • Analytics and reader engagement tracking

Privacy Requirements

  • GDPR compliance for European readers
  • Cookie consent for analytics and personalization
  • Privacy policy detailing data collection
  • Reader data protection and encryption
  • Commenter information security
  • Subscription data protection
  • Third-party service compliance
  • Data retention policies

Security Vulnerabilities

  • SQL injection in search and filtering
  • Cross-site scripting in comments and articles
  • Content scraping and article theft
  • Comment spam and bot attacks
  • Unauthorized content access (paywall bypass)
  • Author account compromise
  • Media file upload vulnerabilities
  • DDoS attacks during breaking news

Deployment Options

  • WordPress VIP: Enterprise WordPress hosting
  • Ghost Pro: Managed Ghost platform
  • AWS Media Services: For large-scale media sites
  • Google Cloud Platform: With global CDN
  • Netlify/Vercel: For JAMstack news sites
  • Traditional hosting: With caching and CDN

Database Options

  • PostgreSQL: For complex content relationships
  • MySQL: Traditional and widely supported
  • MongoDB: For flexible content schemas
  • Elasticsearch: For advanced search capabilities
  • Redis: For caching and session storage

Frontend & Backend Technologies

Frontend Options:

  • React/Next.js with SSR for SEO
  • Vue.js/Nuxt.js with server-side rendering
  • Traditional PHP with caching
  • WordPress with custom themes

Backend Options:

  • Node.js/Express with TypeScript
  • Python/Django with Django CMS
  • PHP/Laravel with Laravel Nova
  • WordPress with custom plugins

Content Management:

  • Strapi, Contentful, or Sanity for headless CMS
  • WordPress as traditional CMS
  • Ghost for publication-focused sites
